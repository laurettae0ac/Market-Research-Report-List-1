<p><p>Polycarbonate (PC) films are categorized into two primary market types: Type I and Type II. Type I films are known for their high clarity, impact resistance, and lightweight, making them ideal for applications such as safety goggles, electronic displays, and packaging. Type II films, on the other hand, offer superior thermal stability and UV resistance, making them suitable for outdoor applications like signage and construction materials. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and telecommunications, meeting varying performance requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>The High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) film market is primarily divided into two types based on visual properties: translucent and opaque. Translucent HDPE films allow partial light transmission, making them suitable for applications like protective covers and food packaging where visibility of contents is beneficial. In contrast, opaque HDPE films block light completely, providing privacy and UV protection, ideal for products requiring shielding from environmental factors. Both types cater to diverse industries, including agriculture, packaging, and construction.</p></p>

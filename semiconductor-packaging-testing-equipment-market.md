<p><p>The semiconductor packaging testing equipment market encompasses various specialized types. Chip bonding equipment facilitates the connection of semiconductor chips to their substrates, while inspection and cutting equipment ensures precise quality control and resizing of materials. Packaging equipment involves encasing chips for protection and functionality, and wire bonding equipment connects the chip to its package using fine wires. Electroplating equipment applies metallic coatings for enhanced electrical performance. Other equipment types may include thermal management tools and laser marking devices, supporting diverse packaging needs.</p></p>

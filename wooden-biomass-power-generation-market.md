<p><p>Wooden biomass power generation encompasses several technologies for converting biomass into energy. Combustion technology involves directly burning wood to produce steam for electricity generation. Co-firing blends biomass with fossil fuels in existing power plants, enhancing sustainability while utilizing existing infrastructure. Cogeneration, or combined heat and power (CHP), simultaneously generates electricity and useful heat from biomass, maximizing efficiency. These technologies contribute to renewable energy goals, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, and promote sustainable forest management practices.</p></p>

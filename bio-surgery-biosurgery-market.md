<p><p>The biosurgery market encompasses various products that enhance surgical outcomes. Surgical sealants provide adhesion and sealing for tissues, while hemostatic agents significantly control bleeding. Adhesive barriers prevent tissue adhesion, and soft-tissue attachments are utilized for securely connecting tissues. Biological meshes support tissue regeneration, while allografts and xenografts offer biological solutions for grafting. Bone-graft substitutions are materials that replace or enhance bone structure. Together, these types streamline surgical procedures and improve patient recovery.</p></p>

Single channel units allow one vacuum line to pass through, ideal for simple applications requiring minimal fluid transfer. In contrast, dual channel vacuum rotary unions accommodate two separate channels, enabling simultaneous handling of multiple fluids or gases, which is beneficial for more complex systems. These distinctions cater to varied industrial needs and enhance operational efficiency in machinery.</p></p>
